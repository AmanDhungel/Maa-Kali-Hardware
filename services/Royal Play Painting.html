Royal Play Paints


Description

Royale Play is a translucent paint, meaning you can see two shades at the same time, giving a two-tone finish. We use a two coat system, base coat of Royale and top coat of Royale Play. Being spoilt for choice can be a good thing and Royale Play will do just that. This water-based special effects paint offers one of the largest variety of effects for interior walls to choose from.

Types of Royal Play Effects
  • Toorent
  • Fizz
  • Seashell
  • delta
  • and Many more....


Benefit of Royal Play
  • special effects
  • easy application
  • sheen
  • co-polymer, emulsion based, environment friendly
